# 1. Product Overview

## Problem Statement
One of the most common daily household questions in Indian families is:

> “Aaj khane mein kya banana hai?”

This decision becomes more stressful for:
- working professionals
- homemakers
- mothers managing family responsibilities
- people balancing health, budget, and time

Many families also struggle with:
- repetitive meals
- unused vegetables getting wasted
- lack of meal planning
- balancing nutrition and taste
- regional food preferences
- managing cooking time

The goal of this product is to simplify meal planning using AI and personalization.

---

# 2. Vision

To create an AI-powered smart meal planning assistant for Indian households that generates personalized daily and weekly meal plans based on:
- region
- city
- veg/non-veg preference
- family size
- vegetables already available at home
- cooking time
- budget
- health preferences

- Veg / Non-Veg preference
